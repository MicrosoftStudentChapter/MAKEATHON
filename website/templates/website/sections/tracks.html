Social Equanimity


Code against Prejudice, Innovate for Justice and Build for Equality. There is no space for age-old stereotypes and practices in the new technological era.

Problem Statement 1: Social Equality essentially revolves around the fact that everyone must get the same opportunities and resources. But a lot of the time the decisions, especially pertaining to promotions or demotions in a company, are heavily affected by the biases and stereotypes that already exist consciously or subconsciously in our minds. So to prevent such unfair inclinations, develop and implement an algorithm in the form of software that records the performance of a person upon multiple factors and suggests the name of employees along with their performance report to a higher authority for promotions or demotions.

Problem Statement 2: Sometimes we forget about people with disabilities. A lot of times their voice gets lost among the masses regarding their issues, especially when they need a job to support themselves and their families. Taking such a problem into account design a software(website/app) that would enable each and every disabled person to get registered and to seek jobs based on their potential abilities at one click in various government agencies/offices.
